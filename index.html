Privacy Policy for GlobalDiary Travel App (EU Version)

This Privacy Policy outlines how GlobalDiary travel app (hereafter referred to as "the App") collects, uses, and discloses personal information, in compliance with the European General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). The App is committed to protecting the privacy of our users and complying with all applicable privacy laws and regulations.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information that is necessary for us to provide our services to you. This may include your name, email address, phone number, and location data. We only collect information that is necessary, relevant, and adequate for the purpose for which it is processed.

When you use the GlobalDiary App, we also automatically collect certain information about your device, including your IP address, browser type, device type, operating system, and other technical information. This information is collected for technical and analytical purposes and is necessary for the proper functioning of the App.

Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal information based on one or more of the following legal bases:

  • Consent: We may process your information if you have given us your consent to do so.
  • Legitimate interests: We may process your information for our legitimate interests, such as improving our App, marketing our services, and protecting our rights and interests.
  • Legal obligations: We may process your information to comply with legal obligations, such as responding to a court order or subpoena.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide our services to you, including helping you discover new travel locations and ideas. We may also use your information to communicate with you about our services, to improve the GlobalDiary App, and to personalize your experience.

We may use your information for marketing purposes, but only if you have given us your consent. You can always opt-out of receiving marketing messages by changing your preferences in the GlobalDiary App or by contacting us.

We do not tolerate objectionable content or abusive behavior from our users. Any user-generated content that violates our terms of use, which must make it clear that there is no tolerance for objectionable content or abusive users, will be removed from the App. We reserve the right to suspend or terminate the account of any user who violates our terms of use or engages in objectionable content or abusive behavior.

Sharing Your Information

We may share your personal information with third-party service providers that help us provide our services to you. These service providers may include marketing and analytics providers, and hosting providers. We only share the information that is necessary for them to provide their services to us.

We may disclose your information if required by law, such as in response to a court order or subpoena, or if we believe that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights or the safety of our users.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. We have implemented technical and organizational measures to ensure a level of security appropriate to the risk, including encryption, access controls, and regular security assessments.

Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • Right of access: You have the right to access your personal information that we process.
  • Right to rectification: You have the right to request that we correct any inaccuracies in your personal information.
  • Right to erasure: You have the right to request that we delete your personal information, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Right to restriction of processing: You have the right to request that we limit the processing of your personal information.
  • Right to data portability: You have the right to receive your personal information in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.
